    BirdBox :: Proxgy

    Adjustable height.

    10 to 15 meters adjustable height at the turn of a dial.

    8MP dual camera.

    To capture high definition imagery, BirdBox is equipped with two 8 megapixel cameras.

    Proximity sensor

    Proximity sensor on top of the extendable camera tower doesn't let the camera get damaged by any overhead articles.

    360° A/V recording and streaming.

    BirdBox records and streams a 360° audio and video from the bird's eye perspective.

    Plenty of storage.

    Recorded imagery for upto 8 days gets stored on the onboard storage.

    Multi layered protection.

    The electronic components are safely and securely installed inside the BirdBox such that tampering with any of them is near impossible.

    Self wipe mode.

    In case the BirdBox detects any serious tampering or damage inflicted on its electronics, the “Self Wipe Mode” gets automatically activated, where the storage is wiped clean and the intruder cannot get hold of any information.

    Multi level security access.

    The only control panel to use the BirdBox is accessible after unlocking the rotatory number lock on the cover lid of the control panel.

    Controls

    Intuitive controls for easy handling of the device.

    Stability in all terrains.

    With the multi-functional levelers, BirdBox can be deployed on the most uneven surfaces without worrying about its stability.

    Built to last.

    Due to its long duration outdoor use, BirdBox is IP68 rated weatherproof device built for rugged usage.

    Long battery life.

    The LiFePO4 battery makes sure the BirdBox keeps running non-stop for upto 8 days at a stretch.

    Weatherproof charger.

    For times when BirdBox needs to be deployed for longer than usual durations, it can be connected to an auxiliary power source via the weatherproof connector provided on-board. This connector also doubles up as its charger.

    Easy portability.

    Along with wheels and an extendable handle to move the BirdBox around like a trolley, it also comes with an attachment to carry it around as a backpack.

    GPS L1 Support

    IRNSS with S-Band Support

    Multiple connectivity options.

    With WiFi, 4G, GPS L1 and IRNSS support, BirdBox stays connected even in the remotest of the conditions.

    Too quiet to be true.

    Once deployed, BirdBox operates in absolute silence.
