    Lockator :: Proxgy

    The Lockator, by PROXGY, has inbuilt GPS and tamper monitor which provides real time location and health data to the dashboard. Helping businesses to track and detect asset conditions in real time. Lockator gives the option to define routes and mark checkpoints, deviation from which can alert the supervisors to take appropriate action. Location tracking, tamper alerts and status logs offer an additional layer of safety while transporting business equipment.



















    IP 68 rated.

    Waterproof. Dustproof. Weatherproof.

    Multiple ways to unlock.

    Using NFC, RFiD, mobile application or a predefined radio signal are some of the ways one can unlock the Lockator in a safe and secure manner.

    Reinforced steel threading.

    Industry grade steel threading ensures that the lock cant be broken easily. The mechanism is also safe from any external short circuiting attempts.

    USB type-C charging.

    For faster charging, c-type USB port allows the Lockator to get charged on the move.

    No network?
    No problem.

    Lockator 2 links with the geolocation satellites and stays connected even in the remotest of areas.

    Geo-location.
    Geo-fencing.

    Get greater control by defining geo-locations and limiting the lock’s opening within a pre-defined area. Role based authorizations ensure higher flexibility.

    Tamper detection electronics.

    Lockator is a tamper detection intelligent product which alerts you in case of any tampering detected at PCB or mechanical level.

    Upgradable

    eSIM

    BlueTooth

    Multiple connectivity options.

    An industry first with physical SIM and eSIM slots for cross network connectivity. 5G available in Lockator 2.
