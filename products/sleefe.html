    Sleefe :: Proxgy

    Revolutionary Smart Wearable.

    PROXGY has developed Sleefe to provide immersive, handsfree, intelligent, safe, analytical solutions to blue collared work force to connect, collaborate and coordinate.

    Perfect Point of View AV communication.

    Front camera with Infrared Sensor for low-light image capturing and video recording ability.

    Crystal clear image.

    Video streaming and recording upto full HD 1920X1080

    Up to 16 channels.

    With accessible ergonomic radio switch right below the helm of the cap, switching between radio communication channels is as easy as it gets for the user.

    Safety Titanium clip.

    Easily slides and sits comfortably onto any baseball cap. The titanium clip ensures a long lasting and durable usage, tested for over a million cycles.

    Loud. Clear.

    In built Mic and speakers for audio communication. A dedicated volume button is also provided so no matter how loud the environment gets, Sleefe always delivers.

    Ease of use.

    Tactile buttons for easy access and usability

    Faster charging.

    USB type-C for faster charging.
